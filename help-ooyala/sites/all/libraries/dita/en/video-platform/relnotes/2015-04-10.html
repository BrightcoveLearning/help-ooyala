2015-04-10 Release

Notable changes for this release include a new integration with Amazon Fire TV.

Ooyala Integration with Amazon Fire TV

We are excited to announce support for Amazon Fire TV HTML5 web apps, which allows you to quickly and easily syndicate content to the Amazon Fire TV line of devices with Ooyala.

Ooyala now provides a quick and easy way to publish content from Backlot using Universal Syndication Feeds to Amazon Fire TV. The feed from Backlot containing content and metadata is transformed into content categories on the Amazon Fire TV interface, where end users can select content categories of their choice and view all videos related to that category on Fire TV.
