Player V3 Maximum Bit Rate for the Flash Player (Deprecated)

Generally, the smaller the embed width of a video, the more likely the video is being served by a device that has a less powerful CPU or a lower bandwidth connection.

The following table describes the default maximum bit rate served, based on the embed width of the player.

Embed Width Maximum Bit Rate Served
800 and above No limit
600 to 799 2500
400 to 599 1600
280 to 399 800
0 to 279 500
Note: In the Backlot UI, the video preview plays content at a bit rate optimized for your current connection.
