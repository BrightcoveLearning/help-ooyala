Using the Ooyala Help Center

The Ooyala Help Center is where you find Ooyala documentation, software downloads, video tutorials, access to articles in the Ooyala Community, and a quick link to Ooyala Technical Support.

Launching the Help Center

To launch the Help Center, go to help-ooyala.brightcove.com.

Help Center Home

Navigating the Help Center

To navigate the Help Center, use the horizontal navigation bar at the top of the page.

Navigating Guides

  1. In the Help Center navigation bar, choose Guides
  2. Choose a product.
  3. In the left navigation bar, expand the list.
  4. In the left navigation bar, choose a guide or topic. Expand and collapse the list as needed.
