Channel Create Summary

Review your channel configuration and create your channel from this page.

Review the Summary information and then click Create Channel.

This action returns you to the Channels tab, which displays the status of the Channel.

The channel may take a few minutes to set up. While it is being created, the channel displays a default logo on the left. When the channel is running, the channel displays a thumbnail of its streams as the logo.

Important: Digital Rights Management is currently not available for sub-channels within a High Availability (HA) channel.
