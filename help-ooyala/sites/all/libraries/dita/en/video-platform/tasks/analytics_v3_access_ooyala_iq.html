Accessing Ooyala IQ

Access Ooyala IQ through Backlot. Log into Backlot and click ANALYZE.

OR

You can access Ooyala IQ at http://analytics.ooyala.com and login with your Backlot credentials.

Note: The Ooyala IQ user interface has been translated to Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Korean, Japanese, Simplified Chinese, and Traditional Chinese. Make your language selection when you log into Ooyala IQ.

Forgotten Username or Password

Contact your Ooyala Customer Success Manager if you forget your Backlot username. See Changing Your Password if you forget your password.
