Exporting Business Intelligence Reports

You can export a CSV report containing all or selective reports from the Business Intelligence page. Reports can contain up to 1,000 rows.
  1. Click Export Options at the top right of the Business Intelligence page.
  2. (Optional) By default, all metrics are selected for all tabs (Performance, Engagement, Reach, Playthrough, and Discovery). Deselect any reports or metrics you do not want in your report.
  3. Click Export selected options. A CSV report containing the selected options will generate and download on your computer.
