Masking the Player URL

You can change all instances of Ooyala URLs from player.ooyala.com to your own web site subdomain (player.mycompany.com, video.mycompany.com, content.mycompany.com, and so on..

Note: This option is not supported for Player V3.

To mask the Player URL:

  1. Contact your webmaster.
  2. Ask your webmaster to create a redirect from your subdomain to player.ooyala.com.

    For example, they might add the following entry in Apache:

    RedirectMatch player\.mydomain\.com/(.*) player.ooyala.com/$1
  3. Make sure your CDN is updated to serve requests for the new subdomain.
