Edit a Playlist

Add or delete videos from your palylist while the channel is running or stopped.

Any prerequisites for completing the task.
Provide context for the task you are about to explain.
  1. Optional: If the channel associated with the playlist is running, and you want to change the first or second asset playing, stop the channel.
  2. In Ooyala Live, click the Playlist tab.
  3. Click the playlist.
  4. On the Playlist details page, click the Edit button.
  5. In the Edit Playlist window that opens, you can scroll, drag and drop videos, or delete videos using the trashcan icon.
  6. When you finish, click the Apply button.
