Ooyala IQ Workspace Reference

Your workspace in Ooyala IQ has several parts.

This section is a reference for all the features in the Ooyala IQ user interface. The visible workspace in Ooyala IQ is divided into several sections, each dealing with a different kind of data, view of data, or function.

  • Ooyala IQ Dashboard
    Use the analytics dashboard, an enhancement of Ooyala IQ, to see what is happening with your content in real time so you can make decisions and take immediate action.
  • Business Intelligence Reports
    Use the Business Intelligence page to view details in your data filtered in various ways to isolate specific points of interest.
  • Video Details
    Use the Video Details page to investigate video performance.
