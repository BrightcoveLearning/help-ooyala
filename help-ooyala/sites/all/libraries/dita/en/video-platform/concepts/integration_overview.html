Overview

To increase engagement with your content, Ooyala provides code samples and other tools to enable you to integrate your content with other environments and services.

For example, Ooyala currently supports the following:

  • First party ad module (Ooyala Pulse)
  • Third party ad modules, including LiveRail, FreeWheel, Google IMA, and VAST and VPAID
  • Twitter
  • Facebook
  • iOS Apps
  • Android Apps
  • Native Android (Hook)
  • Multiple programming languages
  • Multiple content management systems (CMSes)
