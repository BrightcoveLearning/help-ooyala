Player V4 Ad Integration

Player V4 uses a new Ad Manager Framework to integrate with ad managers and create plugins for IAB standards.

See the Player V4 documentation for full details on how the Ad Manager Framework and different ad plugins work with Player V4.

Player V4 supports the following ad integrations and IAB standards.

  • FreeWheel Ad Plugin
  • Google IMA Ad Plugin (DFP)
  • Ooyala Pulse Ad Plugin
  • VAST and VPAID Ad Plugin (includes LiveRail - VAST ads only)
  • VAST 2.0 and 3.0 through the VAST and VPAID Ad Plugin
  • VPAID 1.0 for Google IMA through the Google IMA Ad Plugin
  • VPAID 2.0 through the VAST and VPAID Ad Plugin
  • VMAP 1 through the VAST and VPAID Ad Plugin
