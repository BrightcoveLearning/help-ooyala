Online Media: How Are My Different Brands Performing?

Context: Your company has many different brands, so you use a different player for each brand. You want to see how each brand is performing.

How To in Ooyala IQ

Filter the Business Intelligence page by player and then analyze the metrics for each player.
  1. Go to the Business Intelligence page.
  2. Scroll down to the Player dimension panel.
  3. Select List mode.
  4. Click the Filter icon at the top right of the Player panel.
  5. Select the checkbox next to the first player (brand) you want to analyze.
  6. Click Apply.
  7. Look at the top video content for this player in the Asset panel.
  8. See what country had the top number of plays for the player in the Geography panel.
  9. Observe any other relevant metrics.
  10. Deselect the player (brand) and repeat steps 2-10 for each player you want to analyze.
