Pods

Pods display your custom playlists with the player.

Pods are invisible containers for playlists. Pods can contain one or more playlists, and can be customized to display playlists in different positions with respect to the player.
Note: Playlists are not visible by default. If you do not enable pods for your player, playlists are not visible to users. To make playlists visible to users, enable pods by associating the playlist and pod with a player.

You can enable a pod with the same customizations across multiple players programmatically by copying requests for each player.

To associate pods and playlists with a player, you associate a third-party module with the player.

  • Updating Playlist/Pods Modules Associated with a Player

  • Deleting a Playlist/Pods Module's Association with a Player
    You can delete the association of a Playlist/Pods module with a player.
  • Playerless Pods for ThemeBuilder
    In Theme Builder, pods are invisible containers for playlists that let users view playlists with the player. Pods can be created without a player.
