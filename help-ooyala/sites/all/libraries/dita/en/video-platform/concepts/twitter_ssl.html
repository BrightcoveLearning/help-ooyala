SSL Required for All Content

Twitter requires that all your content be served over Secure Sockets Layer (SSL)/HTTPS, including but not limited to:
  • Ad managers
  • VAST tags or other ad tags
  • Remote promo images

All content must be sent over SSL/HTTPS.

If you are using your own CDN, it must be set up for HTTPS within the Ooyala system.

If you are in doubt about your Ooyala configuration, contact your Customer Success Manager
