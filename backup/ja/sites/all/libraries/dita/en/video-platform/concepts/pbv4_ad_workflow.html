広告統合ワークフロー

The following figure shows the high-level workflow of integrating ads with Player V4.

Step 1: Create Ads

Work with your ad server/ad manager to create ads. See 広告タイプ for definitions of commonly used ad types.

Step 2: Create Ad Sets and Link to Video Assets in Backlot

注意：
  • Required for VAST and VPAID ads.
  • Optional for Ooyala Pulse, Google IMA and FreeWheel ads. If you want ads associated with your video, if you do not associate an ad set with your video asset in Backlot, you must associate ads with your players and video asset via ad manager-specific page level parameters.
Create ad sets in Backlot and associate them with your video assets.
  1. Create an ad set in Backlot with the バックスロットUI or バックスロットAPI. Ad sets are ad configurations that control the ad experience of an asset during playback.
  2. Assign an ad set to an asset or multiple assets with the Backlot UI, Backlot API, or Player API.
注意： With the Player API, you can associate an ad set only with an asset on your web page. To associate an ad set with an asset on multiple playersm you must replicate the code for each player. With the Backlot API, you can associate an ad set with an asset more concretely. That is, when you associate an asset with an ad set using the Backlot API, the asset and the ad set will be paired together on any player and page on which you play the asset.
注意： For VAST and VPAID ads, you must create an ad set in Backlot and associate it with your asset via Backlot.

Step 3: Integrate Ads on the HTML Page

Integrate your ad manager with the V4 player at the (HTML5) page-level by loading the relevant ad plugin and, optionally, specifying parameters to configure your ads and associate ads with your players and video assets.
注意： You must load the ad plugin for ads to work with your player.

Step 4: View Ad Content

The video consumer is now able to view the ad content associated with the video asset.
