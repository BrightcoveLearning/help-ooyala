Getting Set Up

Before you can use the Backlot API, you must be set up to use them. sign up for an account. If you do not already have an account, you can sign up for a free trial.

The free trial includes 1 hour of delivery and 5GB of uploaded content. To sign up, do the following:

  1. Go to the Free Trial page.
  2. Fill out and submit the form. Ooyala sends you an activation link.
  3. Go to your email account, open the email, and click the activation link.
  4. Log in to the Backlot UI. The Backlot UI opens.
