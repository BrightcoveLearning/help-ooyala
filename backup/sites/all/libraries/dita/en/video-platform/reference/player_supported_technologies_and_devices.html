Player V3 Functional Support Across Environments (Deprecated)

Player V3 functionality that Ooyala supports in various environments.

Ad Support

Important: Ooyala Player V3 is deprecated and is scheduled to be disabled on 2018-01-31. After that date, Player V3 will no longer play your video or audio content. Customers still using Player V3 need to migrate to Player V4 (see Migrating from Player V3 and Player Migration FAQ).
  • For supported ad integrations, see Client Side Ad Integration.
  • For supported third party ad servers and ad networks, see Supported Ad Providers.
