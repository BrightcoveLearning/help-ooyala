Ad Set Fields for Tremor Media

The fields described here are for ad sets with ad source set to Tremor Media.

The fields pertain to integrating an ad set with any of the following ad sources:
  • Tremor Media Acudeo
  • Tremor Media Acudeo Lite
Note: Always check your ad source's documentation for exact meaning of a field.
Field Name Description
Ad Policy ID Unique identifier of Acudeo ad policy
Tracking Pixel URL URL for tracking pixels to monitor the delivery of your ad assets
Ad Position Where ad should be played: pre-roll, in-stream (mid-roll), post-roll (Tremor Media Acudeo Lite)
Show first ad after x videos Ad frequency setting
Play an ad every x videos Ad frequency setting
