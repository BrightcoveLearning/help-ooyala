Nielsen Integration Requirements

Requirements for integrating Ooyala mobile apps with Nielsen analytics.

  • You must have registered for an account with Nielsen.
  • Nielsen ensures privacy protections with each App SDK integration, including disclosures and user opt-out methods. Nielsen requires that applications conform with these privacy protections. For details, see Nielsen Privacy Requirements.
  • Before you publish your app, Nielsen must certify your integration. For details, see Nielsen API Onboarding Process.
