Content Upload and Management

Uploading content to Backlot is easy and can be done in several ways. You can manage your content in Backlot as individual assets or in groups you create.

  • Status of an Asset
    The Backlot UI MANAGE tab shows the status of an asset.
  • Searching for Assets
    On the MANAGE page, you can search for assets in a variety of ways.
  • Filtering Your Assets List
    On the MANAGE page, you can sort and filter your video details to focus on precisely what interests you.
  • Recovering from Transcoding Errors
    The error status of an asset usually indicates problems in transcoding.
  • Video
    When you upload a video to Ooyala, Ooyala automatically transcodes it using the settings specified for your account and makes the videos available for playback through your player.
  • Audio
    Ooyala supports the upload of audio assets.
  • Set a Processing Profile
    When you add new content via the Backlot UI, you can select a processing profile.
  • Setting Up Syndication to Facebook
    With Facebook syndication, publishers can easily set up a feed that automatically publishes selected videos in the Backlot CMS to a user's Facebook page.
  • YouTube Videos
    A YouTube video is a video hosted by YouTube and played through the Ooyala player.
  • Remote Assets
    A remote asset is a piece of content that is hosted outside of Backlot. Instead of uploading, transcoding, and storing the content in Backlot, you simply upload its URL and other descriptive information so that Backlot can find the content when needed.
  • Live Streams (Deprecated)
    Live streaming is the delivery of video content in real time.
  • Live Audio Streams (Deprecated)
    Live audio streaming is the delivery of audio content in real time.
  • Managing Custom Metadata
    Custom metadata enables you to add useful information to your assets. You can associate custom metadata with any type of asset.
  • Content Publishing
    Publishing lets you share videos and video metadata with multiple web properties and content networks.
  • Managing Closed Captions
    You can add closed captions to your videos.
  • Bulk Applying Settings
    You can use the Backlot UI to apply settings to more than one asset at a time.
