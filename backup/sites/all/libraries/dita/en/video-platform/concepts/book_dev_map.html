Developer Guide

This topic is a guide to Ooyala Video Platform development.

  • Ingestion
    This documentation describes how to ingest (upload) content to Ooyala.
  • Backlot Asset Management
    This documentation describes how to use the Backlot API to manage your assets.
  • Player
    This is the documentation for the Ooyala Player.
  • Mobile Platform Development
    Ooyala provides mobile SDKs for Android and iOS to enable you to integrate your content with other environments and services.
  • Ooyala IQ Analytics
  • Quality of Service Analytics
    Use Ooyala's Quality of Service (QoS) Solution powered by NPAW's YOUBORA Analytics Product to access video streaming delivery analytics and insights.
  • Ooyala Live
    Ooyala Live harnesses the power of big data to help broadcasters, operators and media companies personalize Linear TV and Live Event experiences through data driven programming and interactive playback.
  • Content Protection and Entitlements
    Welcome to the Ooyala Content Protection developer documentation.
  • Content Discovery and Recommendations
    Use Ooyala's content discovery capabilities to increase customer engagement by providing content recommendations, detailed programming guides, and personalized content queues.
  • Monetization
    Use Ooyala's integration with ad managers and third party paywalls to monetize your content.
  • Social Media Integrations
    Ooyala provides the ability to share your video content with Facebook and Twitter.
  • Device Integration
    Use Ooyala's integration with Chromecast or Amazon Fire TV to expand your reach across devices.

    Welcome to the API Reference Guides. These topics contain API reference information for Backlot, Player, Authorization, and Rights Locker.
  • General Reference
    Welcome to the General Reference Guide. These topics contain general reference information about components of the Ooyala platform including FAQs.
  • Supported Standards
    Welcome to Supported Standards Guide. These topics describe the requirements and standards that Ooyala supports.
  • Archive
    Welcome to the Ooyala Archive, which contains documentation for deprecated features, tools, and APIs.
