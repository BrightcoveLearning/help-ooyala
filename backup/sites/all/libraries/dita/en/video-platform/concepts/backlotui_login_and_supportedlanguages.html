Logging In and Selecting a Language in the Backlot UI

The Backlot UI supports several languages, which you can choose from when you login.

  1. To login to Backlot UI, go to the following URL:
    • https://backlot.ooyala.com/backlot/web
  2. Optionally, before logging in, select your preferred language from the choices in the Languages pulldown menu below the email and password fields:
    The available languages are:
    • English
    • French
    • Spanish
    • Japanese
    • Simplified Chinese
    • Traditional Chinese
    • Portuguese
    • Korean
  3. In the email field, enter your email address.
  4. In the password field, enter your password.
  5. If you have forgotten your password, click Forgot Password?
  6. Otherwise, click Login.
