Welcome to Video Platform Documentation

The Ooyala Video Platform offers a modular, full-featured solution for live streaming and video-on-demand assets. Use the Ooyala Video Platform suite of technologies and services to implement your monetization strategies, obtain incredibly detailed viewer analytics, and easily integrate into your existing workflows. For an overview, see Introducing the Online Video Platform.
